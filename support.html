WakeRight Support Need help? Email us at vince.pavlish@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible. Frequently Asked Questions How does WakeRight work? Set your alarm time, tap Sleep Mode before bed, and leave your phone open overnight. WakeRight gradually fills your screen with a sunrise animation before your alarm, waking you gently with light instead of a sudden sound. Do I need to leave my phone unlocked all night? For the best experience, yes. The full-screen sunrise animation is the core product. If your phone locks, WakeRight uses a Live Activity on your Lock Screen as a fallback, but it won't match the full-screen experience. Will WakeRight drain my battery overnight? We recommend plugging in your phone before starting Sleep Mode. The screen dims to its lowest brightness during sleep and only brightens during the sunrise. What does WakeRight Pro include? WakeRight Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks: Multiple alarms Weekly alarm schedule All sunrise styles (16 additional styles beyond the 2 included free) Custom color themes How do I restore my purchase? Open WakeRight, go to Settings, tap "Upgrade to Pro", then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen. Can I get a refund? In-app purchases are processed by Apple.
